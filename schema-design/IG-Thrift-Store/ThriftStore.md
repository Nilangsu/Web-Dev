# Instagram Thrift Creator Store – Database Schema

This database is designed for an Instagram-based thrift and handmade product store. It supports:

- Customer management
- Products: for display
- Homemade: manufacturing homemade stuffs
- Thrifted: thrift items purchase details
- Inventory: like a storehouse
- Order items: like cart
- Order: for tracking order details
- Payments
- Shipping
