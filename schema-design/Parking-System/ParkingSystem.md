# Clinic Appointment and Diagnostics Platform

This database is designed for a Comic-Con Parking System. It supports:

- Vehicles to store various details of vehicles entering the venue.
- Owners to store who are the owners of each vehicle and their informations.
- Vehicle categories to seperate different types of vehicles for better management.
- Parking Sessions to track when a vehicle enters and exits from a parking spot, as well as to calculate charges and also to allot parking spots based on availability and vechile.
- Tickets to give tickets to each vehicle for parking sessions.
- Parking Zones to seperate various parking areas in the venue based on certain conditions.
- Parking Categories to seperate zones for special access vehicles.
- Parking spots to identify each parking spot in a zone and if it's available for parking.
- Extra charges for calculating charges in case a vehicle uses power to charge their vehicle in the parking spot in EV areas.
- Payment to handle payments based on Parking Sessions.
